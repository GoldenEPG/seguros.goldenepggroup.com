Frequently asked questions about Obamacare

What is Obamacare?

Obamacare is the common name for the Affordable Care Act, also called ACA. It includes health insurance Marketplace options, consumer protections and potential subsidies for eligible individuals and families.

Is Obamacare the same as Marketplace insurance?

Many people use the words together. Marketplace plans are private health insurance plans that follow ACA rules and may be available with financial help depending on eligibility.

Can I get help in Spanish?

Yes. Golden EPG Group strongly supports Spanish-speaking clients and can explain ACA, Obamacare, subsidies, doctors, prescriptions and plan differences in Spanish or English.

What should I review before choosing an ACA plan?

You should review the monthly premium, deductible, copays, prescription coverage, doctor network, hospital network, subsidy eligibility and total expected cost.
